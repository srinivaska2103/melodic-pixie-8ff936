Common Concerns

Everything you need to know before you take the plunge.

🔍
Basic swimming skills are required for certification courses. You should be able to swim 200m without aids and tread water for 10 minutes. For a Discover Scuba experience, you only need to be comfortable in the water.
PADI and SSI certifications are valid for life! They never expire. However, if you haven't dived in over 6-12 months, we highly recommend a "Refresher" or "Reactivate" program to safely review skills.
Statistically, diving is safer than driving. By following the PADI/SSI safety protocols and never diving alone, the risks are minimal. Most injuries are preventable through proper training and equipment maintenance.
